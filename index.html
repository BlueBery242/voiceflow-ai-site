    Does the agent sound robotic?

    No. The experience is designed to feel calm, clear, and conversational, with custom prompts, pacing, and fallback logic based on your brand voice.

    Can it transfer to a real person?

    Yes. You can route by urgency, department, office hours, or lead score, and send qualified calls directly to the right human.

    Can it connect to our CRM and scheduling tools?

    Yes. Most projects include calendar syncing, CRM updates, notification workflows, summaries, and post-call tagging.

    How fast can we launch?

    Simple deployments can move quickly, while more complex businesses need more scripting, testing, and integrations before going live.
